Fried Oreos

Click here for fried Oreo recipe.

This delicious dish is the perfect sweet treat to accompny any ice cream dish. This recipe can be prepared with or without a deep fryer.

This dish is also easy to make, only requiring five ingredients (listed below).

Ingredients

  • 2 quarts/teaspoons vegetable frying oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup pancake mix
  • 1 package of Oreos

Recipe Steps

  1. Heat 2 quarts of vegetable oil to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) in deep pan

  2. Whisk together egg, milk, and 2 teaspoons of vegetable oil in bowl until smooth. Stir in pancake mix until no lumps remian. Dip cookies in batter and place in oil 4 or 5 at a time and cook until golden brown. Remove from oil and place on paper towel lined plate to let excess oil drain.

    Fried ice cream is a classic example of a fried dessert and can be adorned with any topping that one may desire.

    Ingredients

    • 2 quarts of vegetable oil (frying)
    • 3 egg whites
    • 1 teaspoon of group cinnamon
    • 3 cups crushed cornflakes cereal
    • 1 quart vanilla ice cream

    Recipe Steps

    1. Scoop ice cream into 8 - 1/2 cup sized balls. Place on baking sheet and freeze until firm, about 1 hour.

    2. In a shallow dish, combine cornflakes and cinnamon. In another dish, beat egg whites until foamy. Roll ice cream balls in egg whites, then in cornflakes, covering ice cream completely. Repeat if necessary. Freeze again until firm, 3 hours.

    3. In deep fryer or large, heavy saucepan, heat oil to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

    4. Using basket or slotted spoon, fry ice cream balls until golden brown and then drain quickly on paper towel to be served immediately.

    Fried Cookie Dough

    Click here for Fried Cookie Dough Recipe.

    What fried recipe list would be complete without mentioning Fried Cookie Dough? This recipe is the next logical step in our quest to find the best fried desserts.

    Ingredients

    • Prepacked cookie dough
    • 1 1/4 cups of all-purpose flour
    • 1 tsp baking powder
    • 1/4 tsp salt
    • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
    • 3 cups (frying)/2 tsp vegetable oil
    • 1 cup milk

    Recipe Steps

    1. Ball cookie dough and freeze for 15-30 minutes

    2. Mix together flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar in medium bowl

    3. Add milk/oil and whisk till smooth.

    4. Add 2 inches of oil to medium saucepan and heat to 375 degrees F.

    5. Dip cookie dough balls into batter and fry until golden brown and remove to plate.
